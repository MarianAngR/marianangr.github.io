Perfect Production

Perfect Production is a game developed for the Brackeys 2025.1 Game Jam, under the theme Nothing can go wrong. In this game, the player must check de quality of various products, allowing only the ones that meet the standard. But if 3 mistakes are made, the factory will end the player. Throughout the game, the player will be able to help other workers to start a revolution against the factory.

In this project, I was responsible for the narrative, particularly the radio programs, as well as voice acting.

SHUT UP!

Shut Up! is a videogame developed for the Global Game Jam 2025, under the theme "Bubbles" and winner of the "Most Fun Game" by the Francisco de Vitoria University, which hosted the jam. The game depicts a road trip where the co-pilot constantly talks and becomes increasingly frustrated. His speech bubbles obstruct the player's view, requiring interaction to clear them and continue driving.

In this project, I was responsible for level design and programming the behvior of the bubbles.

Los Ojos Verdes

Los Ojos Verdes is a game developed for the Cultura Abierta 2024 Game Jam, inspired by the short story "Los Ojos Verdes" by Gustavo Adolfo Bécquer. In this game, the player hunts animals across the Moncayo mountain range while witnessing the descent of a man into absolute obsession.

In this project, I was in charge of the main menu and the game design.
