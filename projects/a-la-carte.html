Project Overview

"Á La Carte" is a 2D video game developed in Unity as part of a group project for my Master's Degree in Game Design at the Universidad Europea de Madrid. The game is a mix of deckbuilder, cooking simulator, and roguelike, where the player manages a restaurant while dealing with kitchen shifts and customers.

My Role

In this project, I was in charge of the game design, UI mockups and balancing the cards/ingredients system.
